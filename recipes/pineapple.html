Grilled Brazilian Pineapple

Description

Are you looking for the perfect complement to a heavier main course? This Brazilian grilled pineapple is the perfect side dish for steaks or meat heavy meals.

It also makes for a tasty vegan snack!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1 pineapple - peeled, cored, and cut into 6 wedges

Steps:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Whisk brown sugar and cinnamon together in a bowl. Transfer into large, sealable plastic bag.
  3. Add pineapple wedges to the bag. Shake well to coat each wedge.
  4. After letting the pineapples rest, grill the wedges on the preheated grill until heated through for about 3 to 5 minutes on each side.
