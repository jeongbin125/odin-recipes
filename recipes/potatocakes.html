German Potato Pancakes

Description

The hash brown's European cousin. These potato pancakes are delicious both as a breakfast or dinner meal.

Breakfast for dinner, anyone?

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp. all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 6 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and shredded
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Beat eggs, flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl. Stir in potatoes and onion.
  2. Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Drop large tablespoonfools of potato mixture into hot oil in batches. Press to flatten and cook for about 3 minutes on each side, or until browned.
  4. Transport to paper towel-lined plate to drain. Repeat with remaining potato mixture.
